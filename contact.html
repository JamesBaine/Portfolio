About Me

Up and coming web developer with a passion for learning with an endless supply of optimism and curiosity.

One of my stronger points is writing front-ends that are smooth, simple and aesthetically pleasing. I primary use languages and libraries such as Javascript, CSS and Sass but I'm always learning new ones to try to find more efficient ways of doing things.

I am also growing increasingly comfortable and familiar with back-end development using Node.js, MySQL, Sequelize, etc.

Git is my VCS of choice and I definitely prefer Mac or Linux over Windows but I'm not afraid of trying new things.

I'm based in LA right now but I love traveling and going to new places to I am open to relocating if need be.
